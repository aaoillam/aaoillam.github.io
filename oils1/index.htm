How to Use

For more information, including cautions, please refer to individual product descriptions and labels.
Frankincense 5ml:
For topical or aromatic use. Diffuse or apply topically on location such as wrists, chest, and the base of the neck.
Lemon 5ml:
For dietary use. When using as a supplement, put 1 drop in a capsule or in 4 fl. oz. of soy milk or rice milk.
Lavender 5ml:
For aromatic or topical use. Diffuse or apply topically on location such as wrists, chest, and the base of the neck.
Melaleuca Alternifolia 5ml:
For topical or aromatic use. Apply on wrists, chest, bottoms of feet, and the base of the neck
Peppermint 5ml:
For dietary use. When using as a supplement, dilute 1 drop in 4 fl. oz. of liquid such as goat's milk or rice milk.
Joy 5ml:
For topical or aromatic use. Diffuse or apply topically on location such as wrists, chest, and the base of the neck
Purification 5ml:
For topical or aromatic use. Diffuse, apply topically on location as needed, or put on cotton balls to place in air vents of home, car, hotel room, and office. Diffuse and rub on feet for cleansing.
PanAway 5ml:
For topical use. Apply to the bottoms of feet and follow with topical application on location. Rub on desired area for a soothing effect. Use with a warm compress along the spine.
Thieves 5ml:
For dietary use. When using as a supplement, dilute 1 drop in 4 fl. oz. of liquid such as almond milk or rice milk
Stress Away 5ml:
For topical or aromatic use. Diffuse in bedrooms, living areas, and office. Apply to wrists, bottoms of feet, and outside of ears. Blend with V-6™ Vegetable Oil Complex for a calming massage.
