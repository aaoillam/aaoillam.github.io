Essential Oils
 
Desert Mist Diffuser
 
Young Living's AromaLux Atomizing Diffuser functions as an aroma diffuser, and atomizer in one simple-to-use product. Its atomizing technology transforms essential oils into a vapor of millions of microparticles, dispersing them into the air and releasing the powerful plant constituents found in Young Living essential oils. Designed by D. Gary Young and available exclusively through Young Living, the AromaLux incorporates proprietary design principles, technology and features to simplify and enhance the aromatherapy experience. The AromaLux utilizes a robust array of settings, including a master timer control off switch with 1–10 hours of diffusion; vapor volume control; diffusion run timer (1–20 minutes); diffusion pause timer (1–20 minutes); and 5 different LED display light settings. Accommodates 5–ml and 15–ml Young Living essential oil bottles. The AromaLux can transform any space into an inviting, aroma–filled atmosphere.

How to Use

Attach a 5-ml or 15-ml bottle of essential oil to the atomizer top and select desired setting. See instruction booklet for complete usage details.
